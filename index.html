A Breathtaking Dual-Waterfront Escape in North Goa

At Riva Beach Resort, Mandrem North Goa, every stay is defined by beachfront bliss and unforgettable Goan horizons. Our rare setting, where the Arabian Sea meets the Mandrem rivulet, makes us one of Goa's few true dual-waterfront resorts.

From ocean balconies to river-view cottages, every stay opens to a different side of Mandrem. Infinity pools mirror the sky, gardens sway with palms and cafés & bars flow like a beachside village.

At Riva, life moves with the tide — barefoot mornings, laid-back indulgence, and the easy warmth of Goan beachfront hospitality. Paddle out on the rivulet for a serene kayak ride, unwind with spa rituals, then close your evening with candlelit dining on the shore at our beachfront 4-star resort in Mandrem North Goa.
