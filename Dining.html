An Unforgettable Dining Experience

A Culinary Journey Awaits

Riva Beach Resort invites you to dining choices framed by Mandrem's dual-waterfront setting — from beachfront tables and poolside lounges to a 24/7 café. Multi-cuisine flavours, buffets, cocktails, and fresh brews turn every moment from sunrise coffee to sunset cocktails into the spirit of Goa itself.

Riva's Special Experience

Kitchen & Bar

River and sea views frame sizzling grills, Goan favourites, continental spreads, and buffets alive with colour. Indoors or alfresco, the mood is equal parts vibrant and relaxed. Every plate here turns into an occasion, making the Kitchen & Bar a signature experience among fine dining restaurants in Mandrem North Goa.

Hours: 08:00 am - 11:00 pm

Wisteria Café

Open 24x7, Wisteria serves fresh coffee, fine teas, premium spirits, and global bites at any hour. Pastries, sandwiches, and light meals create an easy mood that fits every rhythm of the day. Whether it’s a quick stop or an evening that lingers, Wisteria defines casual dining at one of the best restaurants in Mandrem North Goa.

24 Hours

Poolside Restaurant & Lounge Bar

By day, shaded palms, sunbeds, and cocktails set the scene by the water. As evening arrives, happy hours, wines, and easy beats shift the pace into something livelier. With views of both river and sea, the poolside captures the carefree spirit that makes Riva one of the top resorts in North Goa.

Hours: 08:00 am - 8:00 pm
